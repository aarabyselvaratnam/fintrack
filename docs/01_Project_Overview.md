1. Project Name : FinTrack

2. What is FinTrack?

FinTrack is a full-stack personal finance management web application that helps users track their income, expenses, and overall financial activities. The application provides a simple and user-friendly interfce where users can record daily transactions, categorize their spending, create budgets, and analyze their financial habits through dashboards and reports. The goal of FinTrack is to make personal financial management easier and more organized for everyone.

3. Problem Statement 

Many people struggle to keep track of their daily income and expenses. Small purchases often go unnoticed, making it difficult to understand where money is being spent. Students, employees, and freelancers may also find it challenging to manage their monthly budgets and savings. While many finance applications are available, they often include unnecessary comlexity for users who only need simple expense tracking. 

FinTrack solve this problem by providing an easy-to-use platform where users can record transactions, monitor their spendings, and make better financial decisions. 

4. Target Users

The application is designed for

-Students managing monthly allowances
-Employees tracking salaries and expenses
-Freelancers monitoring irregular income
-Individuals managing personal finances
-Families tracking household expenses
-Small business owners recording daily financial transactions

5. Objectives

The main Objectives of FinTrack are: 

-Allow users to record income and expenses
-Help users monitor their financial balance
-Organize transactions using categories
-Help users create and manage budgets
-Provide reports and charts to analyze spending habits.
-Encourage better financial planning and saving
-Offer a secure platform for managing personal financial information
